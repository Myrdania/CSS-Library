Theme colours

.primary

The primary colour is the colour used in regular text, it will be the most seen colour. It is the base of your colour theme.
Test

.accent

Accent colours are colours that contains less "important" or prominent information. The accent colours are secondary to your primary colours.
Test

.success

Success colours are used to show some kind of success. They're supposed to match the theme in some way, but be "MORE".
Test

.warning

Warning colours show information that may or may not be dangerous. Not enough to cause panic, but you know... better safe than sorry.
Test

.panic

Panic colours are meant to cause panic. You are supposed to think "Holy shit, something is seriously wrong here." when these colours are seen.
Test

.info

Info colours are meant to be less scary than warning, and less high-key than success. It is only meant to inform, therefore it should not be high contrast.
Test
